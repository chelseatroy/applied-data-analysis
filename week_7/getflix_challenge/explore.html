The Matrix Is Sparse

Each square is one (user, movie) pair. Blue = a rating exists. Columns are the 50 most-rated movies; rows are 50 typical users. Even the most popular films have big gaps — most users haven't seen most movies. This sparsity is why we need to decide how to fill the matrix before clustering.
